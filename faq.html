Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How did you find Oscar?
A: I found Oscar while scrolling through an online classifieds website called Kiji. His picture instantly captivated me, and I knew I had to adopt him.
Q: How did you bring Oscar home if he was in a different city?
A: Despite the distance, I made a decision to drive for four hours with my siblings to bring Oscar home. The long drive was definitely worth it.
Q: How did your family react to Oscar?
A: It took a few days for my parents to adjust to the idea of having a pet, but soon enough, they fell in love with Oscar's adorable nature and playful antics.
Q: How has Oscar changed your life?
A: Oscar has brought immense joy and love into my life. His presence is a constant source of comfort and companionship. He has become an integral part of our family.
Q: What special bond do you share with Oscar?
A: Oscar and I share a unique bond. I believe he understands me on a deeper level, and his affectionate nature makes our connection truly special. He follows me around the house and provides endless love and cuddles.
